Our Story

Born from a Passion
for the Plant

Zota Gardens was founded in 2025 with a single mission: grow exceptional cannabis the right way. Rooted in Frogtown, St. Paul, we operate under Minnesota's OCM Micro Cultivator license and keep our canopy intentionally small so every plant gets the attention it deserves.

We're not chasing volume. We're chasing quality — dense, trichome-rich flowers with complex terpene profiles, grown by hands that genuinely care. Our first batch hits METRC and Minnesota dispensaries in July 2026.

The Micro Cultivator Difference

🌱

Small Canopy, Big Focus

Our micro license keeps our grow intentionally small. Every plant gets hands-on attention from seed to harvest — no shortcuts, no compromise.

✋

Hand Grown & Trimmed

From training and topping to the final trim, human hands touch every plant. The result is premium flower that bulk grows simply can't replicate.

🔬

We select cultivars for expressive terpene profiles — not just THC numbers. Real flavor, real effects, real craft cannabis.
